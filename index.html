New York City - Citi Bike Project - July 2022 to July 2023


By Julia Liou on September 04, 2023

The Citibike project, also known as Citi Bike, is a bicycle-sharing program that was launched in New York City in May 2013. It is one of the largest bike-sharing systems in the United States and is operated by Lyft, a transportation network company.
